Lasagna recipe

Description

Typical italian sauce

  • Ground meat (beef, pork, or a combination)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Red wine
  • Beef or chicken broth
  • Italian seasoning (oregano, basil, thyme)
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Sauté vegetables (the soffritto):
    • Sauté finely chopped onion, carrot, and celery in olive oil until softened.
    • Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  2. Brown the meat:
    • Add ground beef, pork, or a combination to the pot and brown, breaking it up as it cooks.
  3. Deglaze and add liquids:
    • Deglaze the pan with red wine, scraping up browned bits from the bottom.
    • Add tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes), beef or chicken broth, and a bay leaf.
  4. Season and simmer:
    • Stir in Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
    • Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and simmer for a minimum of 2 hours (longer for a richer flavor), stirring occasionally.
  5. Finish and serve:
    • Remove bay leaf. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
    • Serve over your favorite pasta.
