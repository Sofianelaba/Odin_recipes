Lasagna recipe

Description

Pizza is pizza!

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives
  • Onions
  • Green peppers
  1. Prepare the dough:
    • If making homemade dough, follow your recipe's instructions for mixing, kneading, and rising.
    • If using store-bought dough, let it come to room temperature.
  2. Shape the dough:
    • On a floured surface, stretch or roll out the dough into your desired shape and thickness.
    • Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  3. Preheat the oven:
    • Set your oven to a high temperature (usually 450°F - 500°F or 230°C - 260°C).
    • If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  4. Assemble your pizza:
    • Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough.
    • Add your favorite toppings – cheese, meats, vegetables, etc.
  5. Bake the pizza:
    • Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Slice and serve:
    • Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing.
    • Enjoy your homemade pizza!
