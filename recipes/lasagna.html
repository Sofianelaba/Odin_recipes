Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna, pasta dish of Italian origin, made with broad often ruffled noodles and a tomato or white sauce

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  1. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • Brown ground beef in a large skillet.
    • Sauté chopped onion and garlic until softened.
    • Add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Make the cheese filling:
    • Combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and an egg (optional for binding). Season to taste.
  3. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Cover with a layer of lasagna noodles.
    • Spread half the cheese filling over the noodles.
    • Repeat layers: sauce, noodles, cheese filling.
    • Top with a final layer of noodles, sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  4. Bake:
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Cover lasagna with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Uncover and bake 15-20 more minutes, or until bubbly and cheese is melted.
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
